Frequently Asked Questions

What is a JFIF file?

JFIF (JPEG File Interchange Format) is a variant of JPEG. The .jfif extension is used, but the file is internally identical to a standard JPEG image.

Will converting JFIF to PNG cause quality loss?

No additional quality loss occurs during conversion. PNG is a lossless format, so the output quality matches the original JFIF.

Are files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any server.
