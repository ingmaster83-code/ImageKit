Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of converting to SVG?

SVG is a vector format that scales infinitely without losing quality. It is ideal for logos, icons, and illustrations, and typically has a smaller file size.

How many colors should I use for best results?

For simple logos or icons, use 4–8 colors for clean, crisp SVG output. For complex images with many colors, increase to 16 or more.

Are files uploaded to a server?
