Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How many colors can be extracted?

Up to 12 dominant colors can be extracted. Adjust the count using the slider from 4 to 12.

Q. How do I copy a color code?

Click any color card to copy its HEX code to your clipboard. RGB and HSL codes are also displayed on each card.

Q. Is my photo uploaded to a server?

No. All processing is done locally in your browser using the Canvas API. Your image is never sent to any server.
