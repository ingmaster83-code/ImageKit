Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF and any image format supported by your browser can be used.

Q. Do both images need to be the same size?

No. Images of different sizes can be compared — the viewer automatically scales them to match.

Q. Can I save the comparison result?

Position the slider where you want and take a screenshot to save the comparison view.
