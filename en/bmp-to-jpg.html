Frequently Asked Questions

What is a BMP file?

BMP (Bitmap) is an uncompressed image format native to Windows. While quality is high, file sizes are large. Converting to JPG or PNG significantly reduces the file size.

Can I convert multiple BMP files at once?

Yes. Select multiple files and they will be batch-converted and bundled into a ZIP for download.

Are files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any server.
