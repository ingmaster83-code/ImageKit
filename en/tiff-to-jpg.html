Frequently Asked Questions

What is a TIFF file?

TIFF (Tagged Image File Format) is a high-quality lossless image format used in printing, publishing, and medical imaging. Files are large but offer excellent image quality.

Can I convert multiple TIFF files at once?

Yes. Select multiple files and they will be batch-converted and bundled into a ZIP for download.

Are files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any server.
