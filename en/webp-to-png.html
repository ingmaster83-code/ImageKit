Frequently Asked Questions

Does converting WebP to PNG preserve transparency?

Yes. PNG supports alpha channels, so any transparency in the WebP file is fully preserved after conversion.

Why choose PNG instead of JPG?

PNG uses lossless compression, meaning no quality is lost. If your image has a transparent background, PNG is the only format that preserves it.

Are files uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any server, keeping them completely private.
