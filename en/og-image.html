Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is an OG image?

An OG image (Open Graph Image) is the preview image shown when a link is shared on social media or messaging apps like Facebook, Twitter, Slack, or KakaoTalk.

Q. What is the recommended OG image size?

The standard size is 1200×630 pixels (1.91:1 ratio). This tool generates images at exactly that size.

Q. How do I use the generated image?

Download the PNG, upload it to your web server, then add <meta property="og:image" content="IMAGE_URL"> to your HTML head tag.
